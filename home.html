Welcome to Astegni, Ethiopia’s first tutor service site. Astegni provides a platform for anyone who is qualified to share knowledge to do so, easily and with more merit. People can sign up to be tutors to help anyone or users to rate and get the most out of it, like…

Being a tutor

Being a tutor is one of the soul satisfying occupation you can have, the satisfaction of seeing your students learn better is unparalleled with anything.

Sharing knowledge is not about giving people something, or getting something from them. That is only valid for information sharing. Sharing knowledge occurs when people are genuinely interested in helping one another develop new capacities for action; it is about creating learning processes.
- Peter Senge

Quality assurance

The quality of our tutors is our core aspect. All of our verified tutors have gone through rigorous screening to give our users the best experience as much as we can. Our report system also will play a big part in filtering fraudulent/bad tutors.

User satistfaction

Our users are our priority, and we will strive to give the best experience as much as we can. We are confident that our platform will help anyone who uses it especially our users who are trying to find tutors for specific subjects, age groups and prices.

Making money

Soul satisfaction aside making money is another incentive this platform provides. Tutors can fill in their prices per hour and get paid from their customers without any commission cuts. This could be a great way for employed people or even students in various levels to get a side income.

Easy search

Finding the right tutor for you has never been easier, with our advanced search, where you can look by age, price, rating, qualification and subject or a specific topic and with our name search where you can find anyone with their name.
